Wizards Announces Pioneer Format

Published October 21, 2019 by Kerrick Long

As explained in their official blog post, Wizards of the Coast announced a new format today: Pioneer. Like Modern, the Pioneer format does not rotate, and includes cards that have been in standard since a certain set. But unlike Modern, it starts at Return to Ravnica, leaving a much smaller card pool to work with. What does this mean for your old Standard decks? How does mana work in this format? All this and more in PioneerPilot.com’s first article.
