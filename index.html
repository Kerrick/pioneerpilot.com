  • Wizards of the Coast Announces Weekly Pioneer Ban Updates

    Published October 31, 2019 by twitter.com/mtgaaron

    Wizards of the Coast's Aaron Forsythe announced today in a tweet that Pioneer will see weekly Banned & Restricted List updates, rather than the “any time” schedule they had before. Forsythe cites the quick adoption of the format for FNMs and weekend tournaments, likely including the SCG Player's Championship. In order to give players the most time to plan their weekend deck lists, the updates will be posted on Mondays. Read More

  • Star City Games Players' Championship Swaps Standard For Pioneer

    Published October 30, 2019 by starcitygames.com

    Citing reduced player interest in the Standard format, SCG is making a change to their annual invitational Players' Championship at SCG CON next month. Rather than being Modern and Standard, the format will now be Standard and Pioneer! Read More

  • Wizards of the Coast Announces Pioneer Format!

    Published October 21, 2019 by magic.wizards.com

    As explained in their official blog post, Wizards of the Coast announced a new format today: Pioneer. Like Modern, the Pioneer format does not rotate, and includes cards that have been in standard since a certain set. But unlike Modern, it starts at Return to Ravnica, leaving a much smaller card pool to work with. What does this mean for your old Standard decks? How does mana work in this format? All this and more! Read More
